Requirements for the BlackJack game

1. Welcome to BlackJack
2. Define what a card is
3. Define what a deck is
4. Create a deck and shuffle it
5. Define what a player is
6. Define what a dealer is
7. Deal cards, 1 player, 1 dealer, 1 player, 1 dealer
8. Show dealt cards (hand)
9. Define what hit + stay do
10. Does player hit or stay?
11. If dealer's cards are less than 17 + less than player then hit, else stay
12. Compare cards and see who winss
